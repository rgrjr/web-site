Henry G. Baker, Iterators: Signs of Weakness in Object-Oriented Languages, ACM OOPS Messenger, July 1993.
An old (but still relevant) exploration of the advantages of higher-order functions (e.g. mapcar and friends) over iterators as explicit state-holding objects. Also has three Lisp implementations of the "samefringe" problem in the discussion of generators.
