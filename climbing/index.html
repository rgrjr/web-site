  • Local rock gyms:
    • Boston Rock Gym, 78G Olympia Ave., Woburn; 781-935-7325. They now have a store full of climbing gear at the same location. (This is where I and most of my friends go.)
    • MetroRock, 69 Norman St., Everett; 617-387-ROCK (7625). (They just opened, in late August 2004, and I haven't had a chance to visit yet.)
    • Gravity Rock Gym, 260 Fisher Street, Franklin; 978-568-1411 or 1-888-ROCKGYM (1-888-762-5496). (Never been there -- Franklin is kinda out of the way for me -- but they are hosting photos from the Quarries Adopt-a-crag event, so good for them.)
    These are just the ones that are relatively nearby; for a fuller listing, see the page for Massachusetts at indoorclimbing.com.
