Greater Boston

Boston Chapter Mountaineering Committee (BCMC)
Committee of the Boston Chapter of the Appalachian Mountain Club (AMC). The Mountaineering Committee provides instruction (e.g. the annual Rock Program) and hosts climbing and social events.
BRG Wednesday Regulars
Google Group for a bunch of us who climb regularly at Boston Rock Gym on Wednesday nights and (usually) Sunday mornings. We use the list to organize outdoor climbing day trips on the weekends.

Local rock gyms:
These are the nearby ones that I visit least occasionally:
  • MetroRock, 69 Norman St, Everett; 617-387-7625. It's an older facility, but it has plenty of space and great variety of terrain, and lots of friendly people.
  • Central Rock Gym, 10 Adam Rd, Stoneham, 781-438-2905. I used to be a member, and it's a brand new facility and nice enough, but it's much too small for someone who wants to climb 3-4 times per week. I also visit their Watertown location, which is larger, but often much more crowded (at least it was pre-Covid-19).
  • Alas, my all-time favorite was Boston Rock Gym, which closed in January of 2019.
Eastern Massachusetts is well supplied with rock gyms; for a fuller listing, see the page for Massachusetts at indoorclimbing.com.
