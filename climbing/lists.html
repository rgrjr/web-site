Local and regional mailing lists:

climbing at rgrjr.dyndns.org (Boston area climber's list)
I started managing this list in 2001 for general use; it is still quite low volume. I will post late-breaking changes to the Tuesday Night North Shore climbing schedule to this list, for one thing. To subscribe, send mail to climbing-subscribe@rgrjr.dyndns.org; neither the subject nor the content is important. To post a message, send mail to climbing at rgrjr.dyndns.org (address reformatted to avoid spam; replace "at" with "@" before mailing).

The White Mountain Report
Every Thursday, Al Hospers broadcasts this summary of White Mountain climbing conditions, news, and weather, frequently including a discount coupon from International Mountain Equipment in North Conway. From the report itself:
Please tell others about The White Mountain Report. Feel free to email a copy to your friends and encourage them to subscribe, it's easy. Click here: 
       http://www.neclimbs.com/wmr_subscribe.html
Enter your email address and away you'll go. You will get an immediate confirmation and the very next White Mountain Report. You can also Unsubscribe using the same link.
